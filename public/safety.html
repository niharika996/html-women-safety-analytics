Safety Tips for Girls

General Safety Tips

  • Always be aware of your surroundings.
  • Avoid walking alone at night whenever possible.
  • Keep your phone charged and accessible.
  • Let someone know where you are going and your expected return time.
  • Trust your instincts—if something feels wrong, leave the area.
  • Use well-lit, populated areas when walking.
  • Carry only what you need and avoid displaying valuable items like jewelry.
  • If you feel threatened, seek help from nearby businesses or individuals.

Self-Defense Techniques

  • Yell for Help: Make a loud noise to attract attention and deter an attacker.
  • Use Your Elbows: Strike with your elbows to cause maximum damage and create space.
  • Kick Vital Areas: Target the attacker’s groin, knees, or shins with strong kicks.
  • Defend with Keys: Hold your keys between your fingers and use them as a makeshift weapon.
  • Escape Techniques: Practice simple moves like breaking free from a wrist grab or arm hold.
  • Practice Situational Awareness: Regularly review self-defense techniques and scenarios.
