Know Your Rights

Laws and Regulations

  • Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005

    This act provides protection to women from domestic violence, including physical, emotional, sexual, and economic abuse. It allows women to seek a protection order and provides for the appointment of Protection Officers to assist in their case.

  • Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013

    This law aims to protect women from sexual harassment at their workplace. It mandates the establishment of Internal Complaints Committees (ICCs) in workplaces with more than 10 employees and provides a redressal mechanism for complaints of sexual harassment.

  • Indian Penal Code (IPC) Section 375

    This section defines and criminalizes rape. It details the circumstances under which non-consensual sexual activity is considered rape and specifies the punishment for perpetrators.

  • Indian Penal Code (IPC) Section 354

    This section addresses the crime of assault or criminal force against a woman with the intent to outrage her modesty. It includes provisions for punishment for actions that violate a woman's dignity.

  • Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961

    This act prohibits the giving or receiving of dowry. It aims to prevent dowry-related harassment and violence, providing legal recourse for those affected.
