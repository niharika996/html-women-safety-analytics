General Questions

What is this app?

This app is designed to enhance women's safety by providing various features such as emergency alerts, safety tips, and more.

How do I download the app?

The app can be downloaded from the App Store for iOS devices and Google Play Store for Android devices.

Account Issues

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page and following the instructions.

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile information, go to the settings section of the app and select 'Edit Profile'.

Safety Features

How does the emergency alert feature work?

The emergency alert feature sends an instant notification to your emergency contacts and local authorities when activated.

What safety tips are provided in the app?

The app provides safety tips such as self-defense techniques, safe routes, and advice on what to do in various emergency situations.

Technical Support

The app is crashing. What should I do?

If the app is crashing, try restarting your device and updating the app to the latest version. If the problem persists, contact support.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Ensure that notifications are enabled for the app in your device settings. You may also need to check if the app has the necessary permissions.
